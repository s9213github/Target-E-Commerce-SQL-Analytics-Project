**Payment Method & Installment Behavior Insights**

**Insight 3:**
* Credit cards dominate customer payments
* Credit card payments are the most frequently used method across all orders.
* Alternative methods such as debit cards and vouchers are used far less.

**Business Impact:**
* Ensuring a smooth credit card checkout experience is critical for conversion and revenue.

**Insight 4:**
* Installment usage increases with order value
* Orders with higher values are more likely to be paid using multiple installments.
* Customers prefer installment-based payments when purchasing expensive items.

**Business Impact:**
* Offering flexible installment options can increase average order value (AOV) and reduce purchase friction.

**Delivery Performance & Customer Experience Insights**

**Insight 5:**
* Many orders are delivered earlier than estimated
* A significant portion of orders are delivered before the estimated delivery date.
* The gap between estimated and actual delivery time is often negative, indicating early delivery.

**Business Impact:**
* Current delivery estimates may be conservative, suggesting an opportunity to improve customer trust by providing more accurate timelines.

**Insight 6:**
* Delivery performance varies significantly by state
* Certain states consistently show faster delivery times, while others experience delays.
* Geographic factors and logistics infrastructure play a key role in delivery efficiency.

**Business Impact:**
* State-specific logistics strategies can improve SLA adherence and reduce customer complaints.

**Logistics & Freight Cost Insights**

**Insight 7:**
* Freight cost varies widely across states
* Average freight value differs significantly across states.
* Some states incur high freight costs, while others are much cheaper to serve.

**Business Impact:**
* Shipping cost optimization should be region-specific rather than uniform.

**Insight 8:**

* High freight cost does not always mean slower delivery
* States with higher freight charges do not necessarily have slower delivery times.
* This indicates differences in logistics efficiency and carrier performance.

**Business Impact:**
* Evaluating logistics partners by cost-to-speed efficiency, not just price, can improve overall delivery performance.

**Overall Strategic Takeaways**
* The business has moved from high growth to operational optimization.
* Payment flexibility plays a key role in increasing order value.
* Delivery performance is a competitive advantage in certain regions.
* Logistics efficiency varies by geography, creating opportunities for targeted improvements.
